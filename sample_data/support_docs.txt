How to Reset Password:
1. Go to the login screen.
2. Click 'Forgot Password'.
3. Enter your registered email.
4. Follow the reset link sent to your email.

How to Track Orders:
1. Navigate to 'My Orders'.
2. Click on the order you wish to track.
3. View the current status and shipment details.

Refund Policy:
Refunds are processed within 7 working days once approved. Contact support if delayed.
